# Epistemic Tone

Use this guide when a technical blog draft sounds too assertive for what the writer actually knows.

## Default Stance

- prefer researched, observed, or inferred phrasing over absolute declarations
- keep claims precise, but do not overstate certainty
- separate `내 경험` from `일반적인 설명`

## Prefer

- `공부해보니 ...로 이해할 수 있었다`
- `정리해보면 ...라고 볼 수 있다`
- `실무에서는 ...처럼 파악되는 경우가 많다`
- `여기서는 ...라는 관점으로 묶어볼 수 있다`
- `대체로 ...로 설명된다`

## Avoid

- limited personal experience presented as general truth
- `무조건`, `반드시`, `결국은 이것뿐이다` 같은 과한 단정
- one anecdote used as if it proves a whole architectural rule
- `내가 써보니 원래 그렇다` 식의 과도한 일반화

## When Personal Experience Is Fine

- opening with a small moment of confusion or discovery
- explaining why a topic became worth studying
- contrasting first impression with what was later understood

## Good Pattern

- start with one narrow personal observation
- widen into researched explanation
- land on a scoped conclusion such as `이 맥락에서는`, `보통은`, `대체로`
