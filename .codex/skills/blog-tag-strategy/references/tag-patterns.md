# Tag Patterns

Use these patterns when tag selection feels broad or inconsistent.

## Recommended Mix

- 1 or 2 primary topic tags
- 1 or 2 secondary technology tags
- 1 architectural or conceptual layer tag
- 0 or 1 platform or vendor tag
- 0 or 1 operational lens tag

## Good Tag Traits

- stable over time
- searchable across multiple posts
- specific enough to connect related writing
- already used in the repository when semantically valid

## Weak Tag Traits

- too temporary or tied to one narrow sentence
- editorial mood rather than technical meaning
- duplicate wording with small spelling differences
- vendor-plus-feature combinations when a broader canonical tag already exists

## Example Patterns

- Spring request lifecycle post: `spring`, `spring-mvc`, `servlet`, `web-architecture`
- Cloudflare deployment post: `cloudflare`, `workers`, `deployment`, `edge`
- Metrics or tracing post: `analytics`, `observability`, `monitoring`
